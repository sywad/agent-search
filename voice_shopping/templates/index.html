🛒 Voice Shopping Agent

Talk to shop — search, compare, and dig into products by voice.

How to use
  1. Tap the mic (or press Space), say what you want — e.g. “noise-cancelling headphones under $100” — then tap again to send.
  2. I’ll search and rank the best picks and read you the top ones.
  3. Ask follow-ups: “tell me more about the second one,” “which is better for travel?”, “cheaper,” or “also check Walmart.”

🔊 Make sure your volume is up (and on iPhone, the silent switch is off) to hear replies.
